Pizza Ordering System for ZazzyZa

This project aims to develop an automated pizza ordering system that streamlines order management, reduces errors, improves kitchen efficiency, and enhances the overall customer experience—especially during peak hours. Zazzyza relied on manual order management, which caused inefficiencies and errors during busy periods. The absence of a digital system also limited their ability to track sales, analyze performance, or understand customer trends, hindering growth and informed decision-making. This application was developed using Python programming language, Visual Studio IDE, and data stored in JSON format.

Sales Performance Analysis for NovaMart Stores

NovaMart Stores needed to better understand their sales data to uncover insights that support smarter business decisions and more effective marketing strategies. Using Python in Jupyter Notebook, the analysis focused on key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total sales, profit margins, and performance across product categories, store types, and customer demographics. Data visualization and statistical testing revealed that education level significantly influenced customer spending—customers with a high school education recorded higher average purchases than those with graduate degrees. These insights present clear opportunities for more targeted promotions and enhanced sales strategies.

Sales Analysis

To optimize marketing efforts and drive snack sales, Trendora—a leading grocery retailer—sought to understand customer behavior around chip purchases. This project analyzed transactional and customer data to uncover key purchasing trends, segment preferences, and actionable strategies for targeted promotion. Using Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn) in Jupyter Notebook, the data was cleaned, merged, and enriched through feature engineering—extracting brand and pack size from product names. Exploratory analysis revealed seasonal trends, such as a pre-Christmas sales spike, and identified key customer segments driving sales.
